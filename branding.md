# Angelica's Brand Rules

Refer to `src/lib/brand-config.ts` for all brand definitions.

## Styling Rules
- **Colors**: Use `darkBlue` (#001B71) for deep backgrounds and `offWhite` (#FDF9EB) for text/sections. Use `brightYellow` (#F7D63A) for highlights ONLY.

## Content Rules
- **No Shortcuts**: Never use words from the `bannedWords` list in `brand-config.ts`.
- **Tone**: Always sound like a family-oriented artisan producer. Be warm and inviting.
- **CTAs**: Use "Join our Table" or "Reserve a Bottle" instead of "Sign Up" or "Buy Now".
